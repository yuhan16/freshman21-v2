Hello and Welcom! My name is Yuhan. I am a forth-year PhD candidate at New York University, majored in Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research interest centers around game theory, robotics, and control and optimization in multi-agent systems.

I received my master's degree in Robotics (GRASP lab) at the University Pennsylvania and my bachelor's in Automation at Beijing Institute of Techonology.


Please find my CV: Curriculum Vitae.



HuskyDev is my blog, where I share my research projects and learning notes. During my PhD, I found a lot of valuable tools and knowledge that not only facilitated my research but also deepened my understanding of other related research fields. I believe that mastery of everything comes from the basics. Besides, the fastest way to understand something is to teach people. Therefore, I decide to write down my understanding of the way to learn new things.

It is beneficial to summarize what I have learned because it helps me build the knowledge framework, especially the way of thinking and learning. Also, I think writing things down can help me organize myself and become more logical and make sure that I understand them. Finally, it is a great pleasure to share the knowledge and keep learning the unknown stuff.
